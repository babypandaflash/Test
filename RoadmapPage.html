🚀 BabyPanda Flash Project Roadmap

Phase 0: 🐼 Preparation

Launch of Twitter, Discord, and the website. 15% of tokens allocated to early Discord members.

Phase 1: 🐼 $BypaF Token Airdrop

Whitelist early users for the $BypaF token airdrop.

Phase 2: 🐼 Token Presale

Presale begins with token bonuses. Participants earn rights to mint NFTs.

Phase 3: 🐼 DEX Listing

Listing on Raydium, submit data to CoinMarketCap & CoinGecko.

Phase 4: 🐼 NFT Minting

NFTs can be minted using $BypaF or SOL.

Phase 5: 🐼 Token Burn

Burn 5% of tokens for every 1,111 NFTs minted.

Phase 6: 🐼 Marketplace Listing

NFTs will be available on Solanart and Magic Eden.

Phase 7: 🐼 Ecosystem Expansion

NFT staking, community voting, and merchandise store.
